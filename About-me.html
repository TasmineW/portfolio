About my Life

Full time student, full time worker.

My name is Tasmine Watler. I as named after the Tasmanian devil cartoon because I was wild in the womb. I lived in Washington my whole life. Currently I am a 21 year old man-child. I say that I am a man child-because I still live with my parents in Olympia, Washington. I am a Team Leader at Jack in the Box even though I used to call myself "The Grillmaster", I mostly work on the Customer service area now. Originally I started working at Jack in the Box in Lacey, Washington while I was in high school. Three years later there was some conflict, I decided to find a new job because I didn't get along with the boss. A new job lead me to work multiple jobs. Eventually I decided to work at the downtown Olympia Jack in the box because I knew that I would get the hours that I wanted. I don't regret going back to Jack. Without Jack in the Box I wouldn't be able to afford my education or my car. Despite many work changes I was able to get my high school diploma and my associates degree. I am currently attending the University of Washington to get a certificate in web development. I enjoy using computers and I don't see myself making sandwiched for the rest of my life. and my associates in arts.
